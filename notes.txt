### 3️⃣ ROI Calculator Page

Create a **simple calculator**.

Inputs:

* Plates per day
* Price per plate
* Cost per plate
* Days per month

Default values:

* Plates/day: 40
* Price: ₹100
* Cost: ₹64
* Days: 30

Outputs:

* Profit per plate
* Profit per day
* Monthly profit
* Payback estimate

Add **3 scenario buttons**

* Conservative
* Growth
* High Output
